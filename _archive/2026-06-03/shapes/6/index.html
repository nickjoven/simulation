mediant: a/b ⊕ c/d = (a+c)/(b+d) (1858 · Stern, Brocot) weights: w(p/q) = K / q² (Arnold-tongue width)

identity

Every fraction in lowest terms shows up exactly once in the Stern-Brocot tree by repeated mediant. The Farey sequence F_n is the fractions with denominator ≤ n, sorted. Two neighbours a/b < c/d in F_n always satisfy bc − ad = 1 — the unimodular condition that generates SL(2,ℤ).

role in the framework

The whole framework lives on this tree:

  • Population law. N(p/q) = N_total · g(p/q) · w(p/q), with w ∝ K/q². The framework is one equation on this tree.
  • Ω_Λ = 13/19. The Farey count |F_6| = 13 partitions dark energy from matter at 0.07σ accuracy. Three numbers — q₂ = 2, q₃ = 3, |F_6| = 13 — set the cosmological parameters.
  • SL(2,ℤ) → SL(2,ℝ). The mediant generates SL(2,ℤ); its completion is SL(2,ℝ), dimension 2² − 1 = 3. That is where the three spatial dimensions come from.
