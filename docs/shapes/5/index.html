devil's staircase — ρ(Ω) at K = 1

K = 1.00

ρ(Ω) = lim_{n→∞} (θ_n − θ_0) / n (rotation number) flat on every interval where ρ = rational p/q ⇒ Cantor support of irrationals

identity

Walk Ω from 0 to 1 and plot the circle map's rotation number ρ. At every rational p/q there is a flat step — a tongue. Between tongues, ρ climbs through irrationals on a Cantor set. At K = 1, the steps fill total measure 1 — the staircase is complete.

role in the framework

The framework's spectral tilt is the self-similar structure of this staircase under zooming at 1/φ. Each Fibonacci-depth zoom by φ² reproduces the staircase. Counting Fibonacci levels per e-fold gives n_s − 1 ≈ −0.035, matching Planck at sub-0.2%.
