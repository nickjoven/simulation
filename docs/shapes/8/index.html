golden gap — 1/φ between tongues 1/2 and 1/3

depth 4 · bracket [3/5, 5/8]

1/φ = φ − 1 = 0.6180339887… brackets converge: [1/2, 2/3] → [2/3, 3/5] → [3/5, 5/8] → [5/8, 8/13] → … gap width: 1/6 1/15 1/40 1/104 … × φ⁻²

identity

The reciprocal of the golden ratio is the most-irrational number on [0,1]: its continued-fraction expansion is [0; 1, 1, 1, …]. It is the last point reached by the Farey sequence — the limit of consecutive Fibonacci ratios F_n / F_{n+1} — and the gap between Arnold tongues 1/2 and 1/3 brackets it through every depth.

role in the framework

This is where the framework's frustration lives:

  • The half-twist is free here, expensive everywhere else. The π discontinuity of the Möbius BC must sit somewhere; the gap at 1/φ minimises its cost.
  • Spectral tilt comes from φ² zoom. The staircase is self-similar under the Fibonacci recursion at 1/φ. Each depth narrows the bracket by φ² and increases the twist frequency by φ⁴ — a position/momentum-like uncertainty relation between gap width and twist frequency.
  • N_efolds = √5 / rate. The number of e-folds of inflation is set by the eigenvalues of x² − x − 1 = 0 — golden ratio. This is the 2028 prediction.
